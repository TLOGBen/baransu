Review pipeline, before and after

Before

Ad-hoc loop

  • Verbal handoff; context lost overnight.
  • Disagreements surfaced at deploy time.
  • Median cycle ≈ 4.6 working days.

After

Envelope-led loop

  • Structured envelope carries intent and gates.
  • Disagreements caught at Stage 1, not Stage 7.
  • Median cycle ≈ 2.4 working days.
