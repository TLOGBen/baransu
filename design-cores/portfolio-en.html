For the past decade I have worked at the seam between print and screen, helping brands rediscover the rhythm of their own stories. I believe that white space is content — when design removes what is unnecessary, what remains is seen twice as clearly. This portfolio gathers six recent projects across identity, book design and interactive prototypes.

Selected Works

2024 / Interactive Prototype

River Timeline

A horizontally-scrolling chronicle of a hundred years of riverbank change, layered like ink wash.

An exhibition at the museum of fine arts using six handmade Taiwanese papers as wall media.
